About

Did you know that the average snus user throws out their empty can in combustible waste or even in some cases on the ground? Well... we noticed and decided to do something about it.

This study explores two different incentive approaches to evaluate whether such prototypes can reduce the improper disposal of snusdosor, particularly in natural environments. The aim is to develop engaging and interactive prototypes that encourage positive user experiences, motivating individuals to return and continue using them.

Prototypes

Prototype 1 – In this protoype, the user can use their empty snusdosa to vote with. In hopes of creating a fun and interactive way to handle the empty snusdosa and create less chance to end up in nature.
Prototype 2 – In this protoype, the user can use their empty snusdosa to try their luck and see what they get. The user places their empty snusdosa in the correct slot and spin the crank twice to see what coin they get. They may be rewarded with a drink, a grand prize, or nothing. This was made with fun in mind taking inspiration from gacha machines in which the user does not know what they will get, making it a fun interaction and experience.
