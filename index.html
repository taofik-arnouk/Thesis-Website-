About

Did you know that the average snus user throws out their empty can in combustible waste or even in some cases on the ground? Well... we noticed and decided to do something about it.

This study explores two different incentive approaches to evaluate whether such prototypes can reduce the improper disposal of snusdosor, particularly in natural environments. The aim is to develop engaging and interactive prototypes that encourage positive user experiences, motivating individuals to return and continue using them.

Prototypes

Prototype 1 – This prototype allows users to reuse their empty snus cans as a way to vote. The goal is to create a fun and interactive experience that encourages proper disposal reducing the chances of snus cans ending up in nature.

Prototype 2 – This prototype lets users try their luck using an empty snus can. By placing the can in the correct slot and spinning the crank twice, the user receives a random reward. Possible outcomes include a small prize, a drink, a grand prize or nothing at all. Inspired by gacha machines, the experience is designed to be playful and unpredictable, making the interaction engaging and fun.
